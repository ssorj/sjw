Lodging

The Hampton Inn in Littleton, NH is a 10-minute drive from our wedding venue. You will receive 10% off standard rooms (1 king bed or 2 queen beds).

If booking online, use discount code 0560061264 (click special rates and enter under corporate accounts).

If booking by phone, mention the Desbiens/Ross Wedding.

There are also several AirBnB and VRBO properties in the area.
